4. How We Use Your Information

The information you input is used exclusively within your device to:

  • Generate and populate the requested Italian documents
  • Provide translation assistance to help you understand document fields
  • Enable OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to scan existing documents
  • Offer AI assistance for document completion guidance

All processing occurs locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

5. Data Retention (Zero Retention)

DocuMate is designed with a zero-retention policy. All personal data you input is processed temporarily on your device and is automatically deleted when:

  • You close the document
  • You exit the App
  • You navigate away from the document creation process

We do not retain any personal data on our servers.

  • No Cloud Storage: We do not utilize any backend servers or databases for personal data
  • No Photo/Video Storage: Document scans are processed immediately and not stored
  • Encrypted Communication: Any optional cloud features use encrypted connections
